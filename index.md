I help founders build systems that are still good five years in. Fractional CTO, open-source contributor to the sustainable web, and photographer. Seattle, thinking from the Salish Sea.

## Currently

- Writing [wsg-check](https://github.com/ivanoats/wsg-check), a checker for the [W3C Web Sustainability Guidelines](https://www.w3.org/TR/web-sustainability-guidelines/), and running it against real sites, including this one.
- Contributing to a new reincarnation of [Sustainable Websites](https://github.com/ivanoats/sustainablewebsites2) focused on helping people build energy efficient, web technology.
- Building [Rendition](https://github.com/ivanoats/rendition), an open-source media CDN in Rust with a sustainability angle: WASM at the edge, less origin traffic, less energy.
- Taking a small number of fractional engagements — [how to work with me](/work-with-me/).
- Commercial Architecture, outdoor photography and aerial mapping at [StorckPhotos.com](https://StorckPhotos.com).

## Background

30 years of building on the web. Founded two web hosting companies and two technical training companies. Taught at the University of Washington and [Code Fellows](https://www.codefellows.org/). FAA Part 107 drone pilot. Web mapping tools for geospatial work. Currently a Senior Solutions Architect at lululemon, working on Digital Asset Management, Social Impact and AI systems.
